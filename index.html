How to play?

"Roll The Dice" is an exciting and competitive game that will keep you on the edge of your seat. In this two-player game, your goal is to be the first to reach a total score of 100. The game is played with a virtual dice, and each player takes turns rolling the dice. The gameplay revolves around two buttons: "Roll the Dice" and "Pass the Dice."

When it's your turn, you can click on the "Roll the Dice" button as many times as you want. With each click, your score accumulates. However, there's a catch. If you roll a '1' on the dice, your current score is reset to zero, and it becomes your opponent's turn. So, you need to strategize and decide when to stop rolling to safeguard your score.

To save your current score and make it a part of your total score, you can click on the "Pass the Dice" button. This action transfers the accumulated points to your overall score, and it becomes your opponent's turn to play. The game continues in this turn-based manner, with each player rolling the dice, accumulating points, and passing the dice to the other player until one of the players reaches a total score of 100.

If you want to start fresh, you have the option to restart the game at any time and begin the journey towards 100 points again. Engage in this thrilling game of chance and strategy, where every roll of the dice could make or break your progress.
